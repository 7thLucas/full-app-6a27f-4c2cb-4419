# Design Guidelines

## Layout
- Single full-viewport-height hero section
- Content centered both vertically and horizontally

## Typography
- Hero heading: large, bold, sans-serif (e.g. 6xl–8xl)
- "Hello World" as the primary text

## Colors
- Clean white or near-white background
- Dark high-contrast heading text

## Components
- One hero section only — no navbar, no footer, no other sections

## Style
- Minimal, modern, distraction-free
